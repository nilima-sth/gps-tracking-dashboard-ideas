# Unilever Nepal Fleet Tracking - Distributor Demo Script

## 1. Introduction (The Customer Experience)
"Finally, let's step into the shoes of the customer—the Unilever Distributor receiving the goods. 

When the Sales Manager clicked that 'Send Tracking' button, the distributor received an SMS on their phone. When they click that link, they are brought to this exact screen. 

Notice that there is no login required. It's a secure, unique, and expiring link. It is designed to be mobile-first because distributors are often on the warehouse floor, not sitting at a desk."

## 2. Live Countdown & ETA
"The very first thing they see is a massive, clear Countdown Timer and an exact ETA. 

They don't have to guess when the truck is arriving, and they don't have to call the sales rep. If the truck hits traffic, this timer updates in real-time. This allows the distributor to prepare their unloading crew exactly when needed, saving them time and labor costs."

## 3. Driver Contact Information
"Right below the ETA, we display the Driver's Name and Phone Number with a quick-call button. 

If the truck is struggling to find the warehouse entrance, or if the distributor needs to give specific parking instructions, they can call the driver directly. We remove the middleman, making the final mile incredibly efficient."

## 4. Cargo Transparency (The Invoice)
"Further down, the distributor can see exactly what is on that specific truck. 

They see the Invoice Number and a summary of the cargo (e.g., '1,200 cartons of Lifebuoy Soap'). This ensures there is absolutely no confusion about what is arriving. They know exactly what to expect and can prep their warehouse space accordingly."

## 5. Ready to Receive (Proof of Delivery prep)
"At the bottom, there is a 'Ready to Receive' button. 

Once the truck pulls in, they can acknowledge the arrival. This feeds back into our Logistics Dashboard, completing the loop and giving us accurate data on unloading times.

## Conclusion
"By providing these three distinct views—Logistics, Sales, and Customer—we aren't just tracking trucks. We are building an end-to-end communication platform that improves safety, empowers sales teams, and completely revolutionizes the customer experience for Unilever Nepal."
