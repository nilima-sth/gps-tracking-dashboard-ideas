# Unilever Nepal Fleet Tracking - Admin Demo Script

## 1. Introduction (The "God View")
"Welcome to the Logistics Command Center for Unilever Nepal. This dashboard is built specifically for the central logistics and dispatch team. What you are looking at is the 'God View' — complete, real-time visibility over every single truck moving Unilever goods across Nepal. 

Our goal here is to shift from reactive tracking to proactive fleet management. Let's walk through the key features."

## 2. Top-Level Metrics & Real-Time Alerts
"At the very top, you can instantly see the pulse of your operations. We have the total active trucks and a clear breakdown of their status: Green for On-Schedule, and Red for Delayed. 

More importantly, notice the 'Alerts' counter. The system automatically flags critical issues—like a truck stopped for too long on the Prithvi Highway, or an unexpected detour. You no longer have to call drivers to find out if something went wrong; the system tells you instantly."

## 3. Live Map & Depot Weather Integration
"Moving to the main dashboard area, we have the Live Fleet Map. This isn't just a static map; it's powered by Leaflet.js and gives you color-coded markers. You can immediately spot which regions have delayed trucks. 

Right beside it is our Depot Weather Widget. In Nepal, weather is a critical logistics factor. If there's heavy rain in Pokhara or fog in Hetauda, dispatchers can see it right here and make informed decisions about delaying dispatch or alerting drivers."

## 4. Unified Vehicles Workspace
"Now, let's navigate to the 'Vehicles' tab. We've merged the Vehicle Fleet list and the Vehicle Request pipeline into a single, unified workspace. 

On the left side, you have your entire fleet list. When you select a truck, the right pane dynamically updates to show that specific truck's details. You don't have to jump between five different pages anymore. From this one screen, you can manage the truck's lifecycle, assign drivers, and process new vehicle requests seamlessly."

## 5. Driver Safety & Telemetry
"Lastly, let's look at the Telemetry & Safety features. We aren't just tracking location; we are tracking driver behavior. The system monitors 'Overspeeding Flags' and 'Harsh Braking Events'. 

This is crucial for Unilever's safety standards. By identifying risky driving patterns early, we can reduce accidents, lower maintenance costs, and ensure our cargo—and our drivers—arrive safely."

## Conclusion
"In summary, the Admin Dashboard centralizes all logistics data into one fast, responsive interface, completely native to Odoo 19. It gives your dispatchers the exact tools they need to manage the fleet efficiently."
